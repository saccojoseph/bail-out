bail.out

no pressure. no drama.

bail.out is the easiest way to flake on plans without being the bad guy. Make a plan, invite your crew, and let everyone secretly vote whether to keep it on the calendar. If enough people bail, the plan auto-cancels with a neutral group text — and nobody ever knows who pulled the plug.

How it works

1. Make a plan 🗓️

Pick a date, place, and the friends you want there. They get a text invite with a tap-to-open link.

2. Vote in secret 🔒

Everyone privately taps "I'm In" or "Bail." Votes are encrypted and anonymous — only the count is ever shown. Not even the creator can see who voted what.

3. Bail without drama 🚪

When enough people bail, the plan auto-cancels. A neutral message goes out to everyone. No blame. No "who was it?" Just clean exits.
