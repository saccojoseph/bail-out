bail.out

group polls for plans

bail.out is a simple group polling app for plans. Friends create events, invite each other, and confirm attendance through anonymous votes, so the whole group knows whether something is still on, without anyone feeling pressured to commit publicly.

How it works

1. Make a plan 🗓️

Pick a date, place, and the friends you want there. They get a text invite with a tap-to-open link.

2. Vote privately 🔒

Each person privately confirms whether they can make it. Votes are aggregated into a simple count. Individual responses are never shown to anyone, not even the creator.

3. Know where things stand ✅

You set how many confirmations are needed. If interest falls below it, the group gets a neutral message that the plan didn't come together this time.

Built for privacy

  • Anonymous group polling, aggregated into counts only
  • No accounts, no passwords, sign in with iCloud
  • No third-party analytics, no tracking, no ads
  • Your contacts never leave your device
